About our Class

Join our hands-on bubble tea class and dive into the art of crafting the perfect cup! Whether you're a boba enthusiast or a complete beginner, our expert instructors will guide you through every step—from brewing fragrant teas to cooking chewy tapioca pearls. You’ll learn essential techniques, explore unique flavors, and even experiment with toppings to make bubble tea that’s uniquely yours. By the end of the class, you’ll be able to recreate your favorite drinks at home and impress friends with your boba-making skills. Get ready to shake, stir, and sip your way to bubble tea mastery!
